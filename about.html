About
Speedy is a game that tests your speed.

Instructions
  • The player needs to click on the shapes that appear on the page to score points.
  • The points scored per click increase with level.
  • In each level the speed at which the shapes appear increases.
  • The player gets 3 lives in each level.
  • The player loses one life when he/she misses a shape.
  • Now you may click back and then click at the logo to proceed.
